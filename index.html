Project Highlights

Discover the thoughtfully designed features that make Dream Exotica a comfortable, well-connected, and refined place to live.

01
Singaporean Design
Designed with a calm Singapore-style concept, the project blends modern structure with greenery and open spaces to create a peaceful and comfortable living atmosphere.
02
Lifestyle Amenities
Enjoy a large swimming pool, gymnasium, health spa, banquet space, and landscaped areas that support relaxation, fitness, and everyday recreation.
03
Connected Locations
Located just minutes from Madhyamgram Chowrasta, the project offers good road access, nearby hospitals and shopping areas, airport connectivity, and close proximity to the proposed metro.

Quality Features
Built with RCC structure and quality finishes, homes include vitrified flooring, granite kitchen platforms, branded fittings, reliable water supply, and security systems.
